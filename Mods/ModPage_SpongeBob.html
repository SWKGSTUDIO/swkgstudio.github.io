Download Bugatti Mod for Minecraft PE: conquer biomes on a cool car from a well-known brand and add speed to the character!

What does Bugatti Mod add to MCPE?

There are no Minecraft PE players who would not like to speed up movement around the game world. At the same time, users can not limit yourself to vanilla transport.

With the help of Bugatti mod, it is possible to facilitate moving around territories using a car by choosing among the variety of models offered. These high-speed cars not only perform their functions perfectly, but also look stylish.

Creating and using objects will not cause difficulties even for beginners, because everything is thought out to the smallest detail.

Chiron

This Bugatti mod introduces an exact copy of the Chiron model into the game. This smart car is capable of developing tremendous speed in Minecraft PE, which allows players to quickly move around the territory.

The developer repeated the original colors in the game, so true fans of this model will appreciate it.

Features

The transport is suitable for racing due to its technical characteristics.

To invite friends to the races, the player needs to add them to his world, where this Bugatti mod is installed.

This is a great opportunity for Minecraft PE users to drive a cool car for free, which in reality costs a fortune.

Bolide

This addon differs from the others by a detailed machine. Bugatti mod adds cool transport for any needs in Minecraft PE, whether it is racing or exploring biomes. Approaching the object, the player will see a button designed to get into the salon. Inside and out, users will see a thoughtful design and a lot of realistic elements.

Players are offered a choice of four colors: red, blue, orange, and purple.

Bugatti Bolide also has sound effects that simulate the roar of an engine.
