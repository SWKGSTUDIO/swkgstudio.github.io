Installation

HOW TO DOWNLOAD

  1. Verify that you not a robot
  2. Press Free Access (It's a gray button)
  3. Complete the steps provided
    3A. If it says, Discover interesting articles, click it, and wait 5 seconds. You can see the time remaining by clicking the X in the top right corner. After 5 seconds has elapsed, press it to complete it
    3B. If it says, Discover related topics, click it, and then click any random one, and simply close the tab that it opens and return to the download page.
  4. Press the large BLUE MediaFire button to download it. Open it when the download completes. It should automatically open in Minecraft. Enjoy!
